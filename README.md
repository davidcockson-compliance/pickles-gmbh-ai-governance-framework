# Pickles GmbH — AI Governance Framework

**Live documentation site:** [davidcockson-compliance.github.io/pickles-gmbh-ai-governance-framework](https://davidcockson-compliance.github.io/pickles-gmbh-ai-governance-framework/)

An open-source AI governance framework for German legal AI providers, covering EU AI Act, GDPR, BDSG, and BRAK professional standards. Built for **Pickles GmbH**, a fictional German legal AI startup whose product, **Vertrag.AI**, is a contract review and analysis tool.

**Coverage:** EU AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689) · GDPR · BDSG · BRAK professional standards

**22 documents across five stages** — risk classification, technical documentation, transparency obligations, DPIA, vendor risk, monitoring framework, incident response, model change management, and client-facing governance assurance. Plus six Phase 2 worked examples.

**Status:** Framework proposal. Not a legal compliance certification. Legal review required before operational use.
